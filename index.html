On execution

Give me a blank page and a goal — I build it and ship it.

Not just plan the thing. Stand it up, get people using it, and leave it running after I go.

01
Tech Trek STEM camp (AAUW · Whittier College). Took over an established residential program and redesigned its curriculum, schedule, and arc end-to-end. Doubled enrollment across three years as director — and the framework is still in use today.
02
DISCOVER Academy — founding-team build from zero at Sequoia Middle School (a California Distinguished School), Newbury Park. Culture, cross-curricular curriculum, and operating systems built in parallel, co-designed with a CSU Channel Islands partnership.
03
Shipped tools — a live AI district diagnostic built on the Anthropic API, a published research catalog, and an open MTSS framework — all designed and authored end-to-end under my own name.

Tech Trek · before (2013 & prior, inherited)

  • 7-day program, abrupt Sunday registration
  • One 4-hour core block; no daily theme
  • No culminating camper presentation
  • Single field trip, limited campus use
  • No pre-program measurement

Tech Trek · after (2014 onward, my redesign)

  • 8-day program with a dedicated orientation day
  • Themed daily curriculum — 21st-Century Women in STEM research arc
  • Culminating final presentation — campers research & present
  • College-level labs — Cadaver Lab, Chemistry of Makeup, multi-day Rube Goldberg build
  • Pre-survey instrument for impact measurement

On clarity

Strategy no one can act on isn't clarity. I build the framework, the doc, the tool that turns "we should" into "here's how."

01
Just-in-time faculty enablement. For a faculty spanning digital natives to 30-year veterans, I authored Canvas LMS rollout docs that met teachers at the moment of friction — not in the next quarterly PD slot. See the docs ↓
02
NGSS administrator alignment. A 25-slide deck that aligned district leadership around the framework and the instructional shifts it required — plus a reusable universal pacing tool teams could adopt and adapt. See the deck ↓
03
Board-level data storytelling. Translated volumes of site performance data into narratives leadership and the board could act on — connecting trends to strategy and resource allocation.
04
A monitoring system people trusted. Built a Teacher Clarity walkthrough protocol that read as support, not surveillance — then built the tool itself to operationalize it.
